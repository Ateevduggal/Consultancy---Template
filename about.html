Who are we?
AD is an ISO 9001 certified company established in the year 1985 in a small town in India and, today, with a success rate of 90%, it is giving students the help they need for getting a job.

With a legacy of over five years in the Indian market, AD is one of the leading players in the Indian recruitment ecosystem. We believe that every graduate or student should have an equal chance to apply for a job if they are lacking somewhere, we are there to help remove that obstacle and push him on the track again. Employing over 800 specialist recruiters across our 20 office locations, AD provides a full range of solutions including temporary and permanent staffing, outsourcing, and other workforce solutions in the areas of engineering, finance and accounting, human resources, IT, manufacturing, and logistics, and sales and marketing.
